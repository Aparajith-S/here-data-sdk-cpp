Detailed Description

The PrefetchTilesRequest class encapsulates the fields required to prefetch the specified layers, tiles and levels.

Tilekeys can be on any level. Tilekeys below maxLevel will have the ancestors fetched from minLevel to maxLevel. Children of tilekeys above minLevel will be downloaded from minLevel to maxLevel. Tilekeys above maxLevel will be recursively downloaded down to maxLevel.
